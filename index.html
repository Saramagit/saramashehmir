Featured · 2026
AARTREC Attack Corpus

A large-scale adversarial attack corpus for testing the robustness of LLM-based recommendation systems — 1,999 attacks across 16 categories and 8 taxonomy layers, spanning three real-world datasets (Amazon Books, MovieLens, Yelp). Generated using GPT-4 via an iterative red-teaming framework with dataset-aware contextualization. With co-researcher Rasha Kashef.
